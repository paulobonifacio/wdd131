The History and Masterpieces of Renowned Painters

Reason

I chose this topic because of the classes I'm taking on the arts, and through them, my interest in the great masters of painting has developed in a very intense way.

Site Purpose

The site aims to provide an informative platform dedicated to exploring the lives and works of famous painters throughout history. It will offer detailed biographies, analyses of key paintings, and a gallery showcasing their masterpieces.

Scenarios

  • Who are some of the most influential painters in history?
  • Can I view a gallery of famous paintings?
